## Overview

The **Formula One Dashboard** aims to uncover insights from decades of F1 history ‚Äî highlighting both driver and constructor performances across seasons.  
It delivers interactive visual storytelling on performance metrics, standings, and achievements within a single, clean Power BI interface.

**The dashboard highlights:**
- Team and driver achievements over time  
- Constructor performance and dominance  
- Driver career progression and statistics  
- Race and circuit-based performance insights  
- Summary analytics of F1 history  

---

## Reports Included (8 Tabs)

1. **Achievements** ‚Äì General highlights summarizing the key milestones of all drivers and teams throughout F1 history.  
2. **Constructors** ‚Äì Detailed insights into constructor performance, points earned, and standings over the years.  
3. **Drivers** ‚Äì Overview of all drivers, their teams, participation, and key statistics.  
4. **Circuits** ‚Äì Geographical visualization and data on race circuits across the world.  
5. **Constructor Standings** ‚Äì Tracks team standings and performance progression across different seasons.  
6. **Individual Driver Stats** ‚Äì Drill down or filter to view a single driver‚Äôs detailed statistics, including wins, podiums, and points.  
7. **Driver Track Records** ‚Äì Analysis of drivers‚Äô performances on different circuits and track-specific results.  
8. **Summary** ‚Äì A complete overview combining top-performing drivers, constructors, and cumulative statistics across seasons.
